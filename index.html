Welcome to my personal webpage

I am a 3rd year PhD student at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), University of Surrey, UK.
My research focuss is on sparse decomposition and dictionary learning for inverse problems in signal processing, with a particular interest on audio signals. More generally, my research interest is on statistical learning and optimization, with application to signal processing.
I am under the supervision of Wenwu Wang and Mark Plumbley. From March to July 2017, I was a research student visitor at INRIA Paris, under the supervision of Francis Bach.
